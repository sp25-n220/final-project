What is Bonnaroo?

Bonnaroo Music & Arts Festival is a four-day, multi-stage camping experience held in Manchester, Tennessee. Since 2002, Bonnaroo has brought together an eclectic lineup of artists, world-class art installations, mouthwatering food, and a vibrant community spirit that keeps fans coming back year after year.

  • -World-Renowned Art Installations
  • -Camping Under the Stars with 80,000+ Fans
  • -Sunrise Yoga, Parades, Silent Disco & More
