Experience the Magic of Bonnaroo

Music, art, community... all in one unforgettable weekend.

What is Bonnaroo?

Bonnaroo is a four-day, multi-stage music and arts festival held in Tennessee. It's a place where eclectic performances, stunning art, and vibrant community spirit collide to create unforgettable experiences.

  • - 150+ Live Performances Across Genres
  • - World-Renowned Art Installations
  • - Camping Under the Stars with 80,000+ Fans
  • - Sunrise Yoga, Parades, Silent Disco & More
