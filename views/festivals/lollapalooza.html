Lollapalooza is 4 days of the world's best music under the iconic Chicago skyline at Grant Park on July 31

It started as a farewell tour – Lollapalooza was originally created in 1991 by Perry Farrell (of Jane's Addiction) as a one-time farewell tour for his band. It ended up becoming a massive annual festival instead!

Lollapalooza isn’t just in Chicago anymore. There are now Lolla festivals in countries like Argentina, Brazil, Germany, Sweden, and even India!

Besides huge music performances, Lolla features interactive art installations, eco-friendly initiatives, and even Kidzapalooza, a family-friendly area with live music for kids.
