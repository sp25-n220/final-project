Who We Are

Joseph Roper

President

Haven Hamelin

Scrum Master

Kiersten Foulk

Designer

We’re a team of three student developers from IU Indianapolis who built this website as our final project. Each of us took on a different part to bring it all together. Joseph kicked things off by creating our GitHub repo, file structure, submitting group assignments, and created the Lollapalooza page. Haven worked on setting up the project’s server, and also built the Shaky Knees page. Kiersten wrapped things up by designing the site’s overall look and feel and building out the Bonnaroo page. It was all a team effort!

Why We Built This Site

We’re passionate creatives who live for music, community, and unforgettable summer weekends. This site is our tribute to the Midwest's vibrant festival culture—and a guide to help you discover your next adventure.
